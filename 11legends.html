How to Play

Football Legends is a game where your goal is to build a complete formation by placing players from different countries.

Game Rules

  • • Countries appear with their flag one by one
  • • Type a player name from that country
  • • Press Enter to place them in available positions
  • • Complete all positions to win!

Features

  • Daily formation challenge
  • Players from multiple countries
  • Position-based player placement
  • Auto-complete player search
